wateats

Restaurant decision maker BlackBerry app

A published BB10 app which helps you decide on where to have your next meal. As a University of Waterloo student, you have access to extra features, such as calculating how much time you have left until your next class, as well as view places that accept the WatCard. You can also check your WatCard balance. The app only shows restaurants that are viable within the time constraints given from your schedule.

The following images are progress pictures of the app during its development

Features

  • Add UW schedule
  • Call restaurant
  • View WatCard amount
  • Set price range
  • View directions to restaurant
  • Time remaining until next event

Development

WATEats is built with BlackBerry Cascades QML, JavaScript and HTML. It is co-developed with Brian Mouallem. I was in charge of graphics and UI/UX design.
